Authored by Anita Ayyagari and Prof. K. Venkata Rao, this paper proposes an AI-driven Smart Hospital Admission System powered by Informatica Intelligent Data Management Cloud, Generative AI, and machine learning.

The work focuses on reducing hospital admission bottlenecks through OCR-based Aadhaar scanning, NLP, context-aware vector search, prior medical and insurance record retrieval, near real-time claim validation, doctor allocation, resource planning, and fraud detection.

The proposed system targets faster admissions, lower paperwork, better administrative efficiency, and improved patient satisfaction.
